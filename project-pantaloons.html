Pantaloons — Visual Merchandising.

A one-month internship building six complete in-store brand displays — from mind-mapping and target-customer research through to final floor execution and photography.

RoleVisual Merchandiser (Intern)
Duration1 Month, 2025
Brands Styled6
DeliverableVisual Display Art Plates
The Brief Research Process Peregrine — Roulette Reverie 7alt — TropiTribe Fusion Ajile — Altitude X SF Jeans — Retro Frequency & Character Shop People — Analogue Nostalgia Street 808 — Graphite Glow Documents
01

The Brief

Each individual student was tasked with developing and executing a final visual display design — exploring tools, equipment and raw materials, and working out a promotional planning strategy and professional photoshoot for the finished result.

The final portfolio needed to depict and analyse: theme, floor plan, fixtures and display materials, props, light and lighting systems, colour, graphics and signage, and mannequins — with every key display choice justified.

02

Research Process — Mind Mapping

Every brand display began with a hand-drawn mind map, tracing associative words outward from "Visual Merchandising" toward each brand's specific mood — luck and elegance for a casino-inspired menswear line, bold vibe and rebellion for a denim streetwear brand, retro nostalgia for an analogue-inspired label.
